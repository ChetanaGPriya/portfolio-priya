Yelp - Restaurant Recommendation System

This project develops a platform for personalized dining recommendations using Yelp reviews, business ratings, and user engagement. By applying text mining and a dual approach of collaborative and content-based filtering, the system tailors suggestions to users’ preferences. It highlights top-rated restaurants with positive reviews, especially from elite users, and analyzes user rating patterns alongside restaurant features. This ensures highly relevant and personalized recommendations, helping diners in Arizona discover their ideal dining spots.

Bitcoin - Environmental Impact

This project analyzes the environmental impact of Bitcoin by investigating key factors that drive its electricity consumption and greenhouse emissions, including hash rate, Bitcoin price, mining rewards/halving events, and mining difficulty. The study explores the relationship between these factors and Bitcoin’s energy demand through statistical methods. The findings aim to shed light on the drivers of Bitcoin’s electricity consumption, providing actionable insights into optimizing energy use in blockchain technology.

Lean Six Sigma Case Study

This Lean Six Sigma project aimed to optimize Gentech’s proposal creation process to reduce cycle times. Analysis of the process—from sales opportunity identification to proposal submission—highlighted inefficiencies, such as non-standard practices, redundant tasks, and excessive hand-offs among Bid Support Specialists (BSS). Using ERP data, the project identified key delays in configuration approvals and pricing steps. The improvements aimed to cut cycle times by 15%, boosting Gentech’s operational efficiency and market competitiveness.
